What happens next?

  1. Application Review (3–5 business days): Our franchise development team will review your application and assess your profile against our brand requirements.
  2. Initial Screening Call (Week 1–2): A franchise development manager will contact you to schedule an introductory call to discuss your application in detail.
  3. Franchise Disclosure & Discovery Day (Week 2–4): Qualified candidates will receive our Franchise Disclosure Document and be invited to attend a Discovery Day at our HQ.
  4. Due Diligence & Approval (Week 4–8): Financial verification, reference checks, and territory assessment will be completed.
  5. Franchise Agreement (Week 8–12): Approved candidates will receive and execute the Franchise Agreement, followed by onboarding.
